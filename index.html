Glossary

Volunteer
Someone just like you! A volunteer (often known as ‘blueshirts’ based on the colour of our t-shirts) is someone who helps ensure every we year we run a fantastic competition. The job of a volunteer is to primarily to make sure that teams have an enjoyable and useful experience doing Studen Robotics, but there are plenty of opportunities outside that including writing software, designing hardware and liasing with sponsors and schools.
Trac
Trac is an external project management system that we use to manage all aspects of the organisation.
Gerrit
Gerrit is tool that helps lots of people collaborating on the same piece of code to review and make comment.
Mailing List
Our mailing list is a way of communicating with a large number of people at once by sending an email and letting it be received by many others.
